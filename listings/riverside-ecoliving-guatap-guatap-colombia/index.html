Overview

Riverside Ecoliving in Guatapé feels like a breath of fresh air. We’re right by the water, surrounded by the lush greenery that Colombia is known for. Mornings here often start with a cup of coffee on the terrace, where we soak in the peaceful views of the lake and the hills beyond. Our private desks let us dive into work while still staying connected to the serene environment. When it gets warm, we can retreat to the hot tub and unwind, watching the sunset reflect on the water. The area around us is pretty laid-back, with opportunities for hikes and exploring the colorful town nearby. We’ve found that remote workers and freelancers are drawn to this place, enjoying the balance of productivity and relaxation. After a day of work, it’s common to take a stroll along the waterfront, soaking in the beauty that surrounds us and recharging for another day ahead.
