Mornings start slow — coffee from the kitchen, bare feet on the terrace, ocean breeze rolling in. By mid-morning, the coworking space is humming with remote workers and freelancers getting shit done between surf sessions.

Afternoons bring pool hangs and beach walks. Evenings are for sharing stories, planning the next adventure, and cooking together in the community kitchen. Waves & Wifi is built by digital nomads, for digital nomads — in one of Central America's best-kept surf secrets.

FAQ

What are check-in and check-out times?
Check-in after 2pm, check-out before 11am. Flexible — leave bags at reception, hang by the pool, or work in the coworking space.
What's the cancellation policy?
30+ days: full refund. Under 30 days or no-show: down payment forfeited. Email hello@wavesandwifi.com.
Payment methods?
Cash (USD & Córdobas) and credit card (+5% bank fee).
Coworking for non-guests?
Yes! Day ($10), weekly ($40), and monthly ($150) passes available. Buy online or at Kooks Café. 24/7 access code provided.
Are they expanding?
Yes — building more rooms starting October 2025. Some daytime construction noise possible.
